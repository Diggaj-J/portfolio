Authors: Diggaj Jain and Aditya Sana


Abstract

We propose a theory based on wave-particle duality and many of the principles of the Double Slit Experiment; Mostly the destructive interfering part. Continuous interference reduces the wave amplitudes to atomic scales, explaining particle-like behaviour without having the wavefunction collapse. Our “Parent Wave” model suggests that particles are localized manifestations of underlying waves.
